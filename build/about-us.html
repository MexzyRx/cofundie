Who we are

We are a property crowdfunding platform, our goal is to help you sponsor the construction of affordable housing in return for a share of the profit after these homes are sold or rented out. We Sponsor homes built using eco-friendly alternative materials which are better for the environment, sustainable and capable of providing high yield returns, they also have lower construction costs in general. Our team has in-depth experience & knowledge in crowdfunding, property management & development & Software engineering which enables us to bring you institutional-grade deals with greater transparency. We are also passionate about creating sustainable solutions to the housing crisis in Sub-Saharan Africa

Why invest with us?

Low barrier to entry

We sponsor structures made with alternative materials and techniques which are specifically chosen because they cost less overall in construction, have a quicker build time and are eco-friendly. We also divide the cost of construction into slots which can be as low as $100 and no maximum subject to availability.

Data driven decision making process

We employ a data-driven strategy, making strategic decisions based on data analysis and interpretation. All decisions are taken by a funding committee made up of experts in the industry, our insurance partners & the Cofundie team. Deals are evaluated based on the potential for long term growth, the credibility of the partners as well as the historical performance of properties in that area.

Security

All slots are backed by a real physical property (Building+Land) The web platform is built using bank-level security, Cofundie will never sell or share your data with any 3rd parties.

Diversification

You have access to different kinds of deals on Cofundie. Which gives you the opportunity to sponsor affordable housing in various African countries. This will help to diversify your portfolio, giving you access to various sources of passive income.

Deal analysis process

Our deal review process is designed to sift through all the noise and find the best and highest quality deals on the market suitable for project sponsors. Our commitment to you as a sponsor is access to the best deals through extensive and careful due diligence on deals and the property developers who bring them to us. We estimate a 5% acceptance rate for the deals.
